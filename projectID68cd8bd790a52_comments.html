Description

Book from 1924 examining whether the Bible should be used in schools. For reference, this is one year before the Scopes monkey trial, about teaching evolution in schools.

Personal scans, so they're not on the internet.

This book was produced by Haldeman-Julius Publications, a small press located in southeast Kansas. Almost all of their books were 64 pages or less, printed on cheap pulp paper, and priced very cheaply. They sold millions of them but few are left, because the paper was so low grade and the covers were also just paper. You can read about the company here at wikipedia.
